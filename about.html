WHO WE ARE

In the heart of our journey lies a tale spun by destiny's hand, a tale that began as a whisper in the mind of Dr. A. Vijay Anand. United by a vision and driven by an unyielding passion for healthcare and an unshakable commitment to the community, what was just an idea turned into reality. With each passing moment, this dream took shape, evolving into the beacon of excellence we now proudly call VR Scans and Labs.
